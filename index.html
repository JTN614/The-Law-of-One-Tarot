Tarot According to the Law of One
The Tarot images presented here represent the archetypical mind as described in the Law of One material (The Ra Contact). These images are based on the original Falconnier & Wegener drawings (1896) and were adjusted slightly to align more closely with the original intention of the archetypes as described by Ra. A special thank you to Claire Dartez for the descriptions of symbols from their book “The Tarot According to Ra” which I highly recommend reading after first working with these archetypes alone as is advised in The Law of One.

You can find the Brotherhood of Light Tarot originally used by Don, Jim, and Carla in the Ra Contact here: www.thirddensity.com

For the complete transcripts and search tools, please visit: www.lawofone.info
